I believe there is an opportunity to build D2C brands for the Pakistani middle-class consumer.

Few reasons: 

  • In Pakistan internet and cell-phone penetration is growing rapidly 
  • We are also seeing an increase in online purchases and sellers, especially major players such as Ali Baba helping build the ecosystem
  • Financial payment systems are improving
  • Covid'19 forced people to move online, so there is more willingness to adopt online based products.
  • There are several categories in Pakistan that lack good quality and reasonably priced products
  • Pakistan’s economy is driven by consumerism
